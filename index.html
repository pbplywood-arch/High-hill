About High Hill

High Hill Plywood has been a trusted name in the plywood and interior solutions industry since 1972. We specialize in providing premium-quality plywood, laminates, veneers, flush doors, block boards, and allied wood products for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Our commitment to quality, durability, and customer satisfaction has helped us build long-lasting relationships with clients across various sectors. Every product we offer is carefully selected and manufactured to meet the highest industry standards. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver reliable and aesthetically appealing solutions. Over the years, we have earned a reputation for consistency, integrity, and excellence in every aspect of our business. Our products are designed to withstand the test of time while enhancing the beauty and functionality of interiors. We work closely with homeowners, architects, interior designers, builders, contractors, and dealers to understand their unique requirements and provide tailored solutions. Our extensive product portfolio offers a perfect balance of strength, performance, and elegant design.

Our Products

Plywood

Strong and affordable plywood suitable for furniture and interiors. High strength and durability for long-lasting performance. Excellent load-bearing capacity and structural stability.

Flush Door

Manufactured using high-quality plywood and seasoned timber core. Strong, durable, and dimensionally stable construction. Smooth and uniform surface for painting, polishing, laminating, or veneering.

Block Board

Manufactured with a solid timber strip core for superior strength. Lightweight yet highly durable construction. Excellent screw-holding and nail-holding capacity.

Veneers

Premium plywood finished with a natural wood veneer surface. Offers the authentic look and feel of real wood. Enhances interiors with rich textures and elegant grain patterns.

Laminates

Premium-quality decorative plywood panel for modern interiors. Manufactured using high-grade plywood and durable laminate surfaces. Provides excellent strength and load-bearing capacity.

Dencified Ply

Manufactured under high pressure for increased density and strength. Extremely durable and suitable for heavy-duty applications. High load-bearing capacity and excellent structural stability.

Checkered

Premium plywood featuring a decorative checkered surface pattern. Combines durability with an attractive and modern appearance. High strength and excellent dimensional stability.

Flexy

Specially engineered plywood designed for curved and flexible applications. Easily bends into circular, curved, and custom shapes without cracking. Lightweight yet strong construction.

HDHMR

Excellent moisture resistance, suitable for humid environments. Enhanced dimensional stability for long-lasting performance. Termite and borer resistant when properly treated.
